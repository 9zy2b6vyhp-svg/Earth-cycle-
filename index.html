The Earth Cycle

Live in rhythm with the Earth

Thirteen months of twenty-eight days, turning around the equinoxes, solstices, and the four festivals between them. Year Day — and, in leap years, a Leap Day — sits outside the months entirely, set aside for reflection, gratitude, and renewal.

Guiding Principles

  • The Sun guides the seasons. The Moon marks the moment.
  • Observe before you measure.
  • The framework is universal. The language of the seasons is local.
  • Record what is true, not only what is expected.
  • Leave the land healthier with every Earth Cycle.
  • Every ending is a beginning.
